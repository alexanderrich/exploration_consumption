Instructions

You will receive a bonus of $5.00 simply for completing the sliders. If you occasionally do not set a slider to 50, you will not be penalized and will receive your complete bonus. However, you will be penalized 20 cents from your bonus for each percentage of sliders over 10% that you miss. For example, if you miss 18% of the sliders, you will lose $1.60 and your final bonus will be $3.40. This penalty is simply to ensure that you remain engaged with the tasks.

Before completing the main experiment, you will have an opportunity to practice the slider and video tasks. You will not be penalized for sliders missed during this practice phase.

After completing the practice phase, you will proceed to the main experiment.
