Instructions

To try to find a better spinner, click "run new spinner". As shown above the "run new spinner" button, there are 30 possible spinners. One third of them have no gold wedges, and always produce slider tasks. The remaining two thirds have gold wedges ranging evenly from 5% to 100% of the spinner's area. Once you click "run new spinner," the 30 spinners will be covered up and randomly shuffled. You can then click any of the thirty squares in the panel to reveal and select a new spinner.

After the new spinner is chosen, it will appear in front of the panel and the machine will run the new spinner to produce a slider or video task. If the new spinner is worse than the best spinner, the best spinner will stay the same for the next choice. But if the new spinner is better than the best spinner, it will be saved as the best spinner for your future choices. This means that while choosing a new spinner can be risky, it can also allow you to improve the best spinner, which you can take advantage of for future choices.
