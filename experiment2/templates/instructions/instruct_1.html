Instructions

In this experiment, we are looking at people's preferences when trading off between fun and boring activities. You will make a series of 30 choices between two buttons, "Option A" and "Option B".

After you click an option, you will spend some time performing two tasks —a boring and unpleasant slider task and a fun video task. The option you choose may affect the amount of time you spend on each task and the order in which you perform the tasks.

For your first two choices, you will be asked to click Option A, and then Option B, to ensure that you have tried both options. For the rest of your choices, you can simply click whichever option you prefer. Occasionally, the options will be replaced with two new options, which may have different task orders and amounts. When new options appear, you will again be instructed to try Options A and B, and can then choose whichever you prefer.

The number of choices remaining in the experiment will be displayed on the top of the screen
