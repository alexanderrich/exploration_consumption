Instructions

In the slider task, a progress bar and a set of sliders will appear on the screen. Your job is to move each one to "50" using the mouse before the time on the progress bar runs out. When the slider is released at 50 it will turn green, showing that it has been completed.

At the beginning of each slider task, only the first slider will be enabled and the other four will be grayed out. As the period progresses, the other sliders will be enabled at regular intervals.

An intermittent, annoying static noise will also play throughout the slider task.

The percentage of sliders missed over the course of the experiment will be displayed at the top of the screen.
