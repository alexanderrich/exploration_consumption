Instructions

At the beginning of the game, the value of the best-yet card always starts at 4. When you take a mystery card, there is a 50% chance that its value is 0, and a 50% chance that its value is a random number between 4 and 12. This means that while taking the mystery card can sometimes cause you to miss out on points, it can also lead to a big improvement in the best-yet card.

Finally, after each trial there is a 1 in 8 (12.5%) chance that the cards will reset. When this occurs, you will see the phrase "cards reset", and the best-yet card will be reset to 4.

In this first phase, there is no monetary bonus for performance. You should simply try to get a feel for the game, with the goal of maximizing your total points.
