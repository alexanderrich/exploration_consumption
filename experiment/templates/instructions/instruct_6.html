Instructions—practice

Before proceeding to the complete task, including the outcome phase, you will perform several trial of the decision-making task without the outcome phases. You can use this opportunity to get a better sense of how the game works and of the spread of possible mystery-card values.

After practicing the decision-making task, you will proceed to a few practice rounds of the outcome phase, which will allow you to get a sense of how brickbreak and the popup-clicking task work. You will not be penalized for popups missed during the practice phase.

After completing the practice rounds, you will proceed to the main task.
