Instructions—outcome phases

After each visit to a room, you will get to play a short arcade phase. Each arcade phase lasts 36 seconds in total. During this phase, you will get a number of seconds equal to the value of your chosen card to play an arcade game called "brickbreak". For example, if your card has a value of 15, you will get to play brickbreak for 15 of the 36 seconds.

In brickbreak, you use your mouse to control the position of a paddle near the bottom of the screen. Your goal is to bounce a ball into rows of bricks to destroy them, without letting the ball fall out of the bottom of the screen. You will gain points for each brick you destroy. In addition, when a brick is destroyed it will occasionally produce a "+15" bonus brick that will fall down the screen. Catch this brick with your paddle for an extra 15 points!

At any point in the game, you can press the "up" or "down" arrows on your keyboard to increase or decrease the speed of the ball. Increasing the speed makes the game more challenging, but also gives you more points for each brick destroyed! Your total points will be tallied in the upper left corner of the screen. Each time you complete a level (by destroying all of the bricks), a new pattern of bricks will appear, bringing new challenges and potential strategies.
