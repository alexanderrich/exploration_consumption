Instructions

Before proceeding to the complete task, you will first complete a shortened practice task where the actual work periods have been removed. You can use this opportunity to get a better sense of how the machines work and practice using them.

After practicing using the machines, you will proceed to a few practice periods of the slider task and the Brickbreak game. You will not be penalized for sliders missed during this practice phase.

After completing the practice rounds, you will proceed to the main task.
