Instructions—decision-making task

Context resets: After each outcome, there is a 1 in 6 (17%) chance that the context will reset, meaning that the best-yet card will be set back to a random value between 12 and 18.

Advanced choices: A set of smaller colored squares may sometimes appear on the map between the contexts. When your icon passes one of these squares, you will be asked to make an advanced choice for the context matching that color. This means you will be shown the context matching that color, and be asked to commit to either choosing the best-yet card or the mystery card on your next visit to that context. After making an advanced choice, a small "a" will appear under that context on the map to indicate that a choice has already been made. When you reach that context, rather than making a choice, you will simply click to reveal the outcome of the choice you committed to earlier. Advanced choices always occur 4 contexts ahead of when the context will next be reached.

"info" button: In the lower-right corner of the context is a button labeled "info". Press this button to toggle a set of reminders about the rules of the decision-making task.
