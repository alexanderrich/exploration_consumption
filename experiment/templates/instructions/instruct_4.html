Instructions—decision-making task

Call-ahead choices: A set of smaller colored squares will sometimes appear on the map between the rooms. When your character passes one of these "call-ahead booths", you will "call the dealer" in the room matching the booth's color and have to make your choice in advance. This means you will be shown the room matching that color, and be asked to click to commit to either choosing the best-yet card or a card from the deck on your next visit to the room. When you reach the room, rather than making a choice, you will simply click to reveal the outcome of the choice you called in earlier. Call-ahead choices always occur 4 rooms ahead of when the room will next be reached. Rooms where your choice has been called ahead will have a small telephone icon below them on the room map.
