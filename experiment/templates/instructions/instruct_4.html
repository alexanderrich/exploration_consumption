Instructions

Once you gain access to the machine, you will have to make a choice before each work period. The machine has a large spinner marked "best spinner," and a panel of possible new spinners.

To run the machine with the best spinner, click "run best spinner." The spinner will then spin and the machine will produce a video task if the arrow lands on a gold wedge, and a slider task if the arrow lands on a black wedge. The initial size of the gold and black wedges on the best spinner is randomly set.
