Instructions

Once you click an idle machine, it will appear at the top of the screen. To use the machine, you can choose to run the current spinner or to try a new spinner. The gold wedges in the current spinner represent the chance of the machine working and completing the sliders for you, allowing you to watch the video. If you choose "run current spinner", the current spinner will spin and the machine will work if the arrow lands on a gold wedge. The current spinner on each machine starts with gold wedges covering an area between one third and two thirds of the spinner.
