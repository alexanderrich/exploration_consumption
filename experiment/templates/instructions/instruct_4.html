Instructions—outcome phases

After receiving each outcome, you will complete a short outcome phase. Each outcome phase lasts 36 seconds total. During this phase, you will get a number of seconds equal to your received outcome to play an arcade game called "brickbreak". For example, if you receive an outcome of 15, you will get to play brickbreak for 15 of the 36 seconds.

In brickbreak, you use your mouse to control the position of a paddle near the bottom of the screen. Your goal is to bounce a ball into rows of bricks to destroy them, without letting the ball fall out of the bottom of the screen. You will gain a point for each brick you destroy. In addition, when a brick is destroyed it will occasionally produce a "+3" bonus brick that will fall down the screen. Catch this brick with your paddle for an extra 3 points!

Your total points will be tallied in the upper left corner of the screen. Each time you complete a level (by destroying all of the bricks), a new pattern of bricks will appear, bringing new challenges and strategies.
