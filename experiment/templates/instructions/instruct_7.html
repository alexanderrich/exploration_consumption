Instructions

You will receive a bonus of $5.00 simply for completing the sliders and holding space to watch the video. If you occasionally do not set a slider to 50 or do not watch the video, you will not be penalized and will receive your complete bonus. However, you will be penalized 20 cents from your bonus for each percentage of sliders over 10% that you miss. You will also be penalized 10 cents for each percentage of video pause time over 20%. For example, if you miss 13% of the sliders, and leave the video paused for 25% of the time, you will lose $1.60 and your final bonus will be $3.40. These penalties are simply to ensure that you remain engaged with the tasks.

Before completing the main experiment, you will first complete a practice task where the actual work periods have been removed. You can use this opportunity to get a better sense of how the machine works and practice using it.

After practicing using the machine, you will practice the slider task and video task. You will not be penalized for sliders missed or pause time during this practice phase.

After completing the practice rounds, you will proceed to the main experiment.
