Instructions

During the remaining time, the game will pause and you will have to click popup windows that appear in the center of the screen. Each popup window will say "Click here" until it is clicked. You will have 3 seconds to click each window, and after the three seconds elapses a new window to be clicked will immediately appear. For example, if you receive 4 points then after playing breakout for 12 seconds, you will click windows for 24 seconds and have to click 8 windows.

In addition, when the ball falls out of the screen while playing breakout, you will have to click one window before the ball resets. (If your game-play time ends while the window is up, that window will be "cancelled" and the scheduled popups will begin.)

If you occasionally do not click a window in time, you will not be penalized. However, if you miss more than 15% of the windows over the entire task, you will be penalized 5 cents from your bonus for each percentage over 15% that you miss. The percentage of windows missed will be displayed at the top of the screen.

You will have an opportunity to practice through five example rounds of breakout and popup-clicking before the main task begins.
