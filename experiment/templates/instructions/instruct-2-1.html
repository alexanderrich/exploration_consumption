Instructions

In the next part of the experiment, you will continue to play the card game introduced in the first part. However, rather than playing straight through trials of one card game, you will cycle through trials of 6 separate card games in 6 contexts: the red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and purple contexts.

On the lower half of the screen, a map will show the layout of the 6 contexts. The number in each context box shows the current sure-thing card in that context. The black-and-white character icon shows your current context. After completing a trial and receiving an outcome, the icon will move clockwise to the next context and begin a trial with the cards in that context. The cards in the 6 contexts are completely separate, and your choices and outcomes in one context will not affect the outcomes in another context.
