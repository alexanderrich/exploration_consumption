Instructions

If you choose "run new spinner", a panel of 30 possible spinners will appear, as shown in the picture below. one third of them have no gold wedges, and always produce slider tasks. The remaining two thirds have gold wedges ranging evenly from 5% to 100% of the spinner's area. The thirty spinners will be briefly visible, and then will be covered up and randomly shuffled. You can then click any of the thirty squares in the panel to reveal the selected spinner.

After the new spinner is chosen, the machine will run the new spinner to produce a slider or Youtube task. If the new spinner is worse than the current spinner, the machine will then simply "forget" it and will remain on its current spinner for the next choice. But if the new spinner is better than the current spinner, it will be saved and will become the current spinner for your next choice. This means that while choosing a new spinner can be risky, it can also allow you to improve the machine so that it is more likely to work in the future.

For example, if the current spinner is half gold, then choosing a new spinner might lead to a spinner with no gold, making the machine definitely produce a slider task for this choice. But choosing a new spinner might also lead to a spinner that is 80% gold, giving the machine a high chance of producing a Youtube task for this choice and a high chance of producing Youtube tasks on future choices if you continue to select the new 80%-gold current spinner.
