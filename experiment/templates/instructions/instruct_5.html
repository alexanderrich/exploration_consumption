Room resets: After each visit to a room, there is a 1 in 6 (17%) chance that the dealer will reset the room, meaning that the best-yet card will be set back to a random value between 12 and 18.

"info" button: In the lower-right corner of the room map is a button labeled "info". If you forget the rules of the game, click this button to toggle a set of reminders.
