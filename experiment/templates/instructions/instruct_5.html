Instructions

There is also a one in six (17%) chance that after the machine runs it will reset itself. That means the current setting will be set back to a random wedge size between one third and two thirds of the spinner.

Below the display of the six machines is a button labeled "info". If you forget how the machines function, click this button to toggle a set of reminders.

You will receive a bonus of $3.00 simply for accurately completing the slider work periods. If you occasionally do not accurately set a slider to 50, you will not be penalized and will receive your complete bonus However, you will be penalized 10 cents from your bonus for each percentage of sliders over 10% that you miss. (For example, if you miss 23% of the sliders throughout the entire task, you will lose $1.30 and your final bonus will be $1.70.) The percentage of sliders missed will be displayed at the top of the screen.
