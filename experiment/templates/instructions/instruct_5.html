Instructions—outcome phases

During the remaining seconds in each outcome phase, brickbreak will pause and you will have to click popup windows that appear in the center of the screen. Each popup window will say "Click here" until it is clicked. You will have 3 seconds to click each window, and after the 3 seconds elapses a new window to be clicked will immediately appear. For example, if you receive an outcome of 24 then after playing brickbreak for 24 seconds, you will click windows for 12 seconds and have to click 4 total windows.

In addition, when the ball falls out of the screen while playing brickbreak, you will have to click one window before the ball resets. (If your game-play time ends while the window is up, that window will be "cancelled" with no penalty if you did not click it in time.)

You will receive a bonus of $3.00 simply for staying engaged with the brickbreak game and the popup task. If you occasionally do not click a window in time, you will not be penalized and you will receive your complete bonus. However, you will be penalized 10 cents from your bonus for each percentage of popups over 10% that you miss. (For example, if you miss 23% of the popups throughout the entire task, you will lose $1.30 and your final bonus will be $1.70.) The percentage of windows missed will be displayed at the top of the screen.
