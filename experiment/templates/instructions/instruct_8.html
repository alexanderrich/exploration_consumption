Instructions—practice

Before proceeding to the complete game, you will perform several trials of the decision-making game without the arcade phases. You can use this opportunity to get a better sense of how the game works and of the spread of cards in the decks.

After practicing the decision-making game, you will proceed to a few practice rounds of the arcade phase, which will allow you to get a sense of how brickbreak and the popup-clicking task work. You will not be penalized for popups missed during the practice phase.

After completing the practice rounds, you will proceed to the main game.

Remember, your choices in the decision-making game determine the amount of time you get to play brickbreak during the arcade phases after each room visit. Enjoy the game, and be sure to click the popups to receive your full $3.00 bonus!
