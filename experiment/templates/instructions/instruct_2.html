Instructions

Before beginning the experiment, you will get to choose one of four videos for the video task: Planet Earth, The Great British Bake Off, Mythbusters, and an Ellen Degeneres comedy show.

During the video task, you will have to hold down the space bar to play the video. When you release the space bar or switch away from the experiment browser window, the video will pause. The percent of time when the video has been paused over the course of the experiment will be displayed at the top of the screen.
