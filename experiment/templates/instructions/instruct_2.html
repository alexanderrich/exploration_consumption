Instructions

For the first few work periods, you will simply complete the slider task and move on to the next period. After a few periods, you will gain access to six machines. These machines won't always work, but when they do they will complete the next work period for you. When a machine completes a work period for you, you will get to spend 30 seconds playing an arcade game called Brickbreak instead of adjusting sliders.

In Brickbreak, you use your mouse to control a paddle near the bottom of the screen. Your goal is to bounce a ball into rows of bricks to destroy them, without letting the ball fall out of the bottom of the screen. You will gain points for each brick you destroy. In addition, when a brick is destroyed it will sometimes produce a "+15" bonus brick that will fall down the screen. Catch this brick with your paddle for an extra 15 points!

At any point in the game, you can press the "up" or "down" arrows on your keyboard to increase or decrease the speed of the ball. Increasing the speed makes the game more challenging, but also gives you more points for each brick destroyed. Your score will be tallied in the upper left corner of the screen. Each time you destroy all the bricks a new level will begin with a new pattern of bricks, providing new challenges and new potential strategies.
