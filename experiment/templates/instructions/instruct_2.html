Instructions

During the video task, you will get to choose among four videos: Planet Earth (an acclaimed nature documentary), The Great Christmas Bakeoff (a British baking competition), Unchained Reaction (a construction and special-effects competition hosted by the Myth Busters hosts), and an Ellen Degeneres comedy special.

During the video task, tabs for each of the four videos will appear at the top of the screen. You can click on the tabs to switch which video you are watching whenever you choose, and can also skip around within the videos by hovering over the video to bring up controls. You have no required actions during the video task.
