Instructions

After a few work periods, you will gain access to six machines. These machines won't always work, but when they do they will complete the next work period for you. When a machine completes a work period for you, you will get to spend the period watching a video on Youtube. Before beginning the experiment, you will get to choose from four videos: Planet Earth, The Great British Bake-off, Mythbusters, and an Ellen Degeneres comedy show.

When watching the Youtube video, you will have to hold down the space bar to play the video. When you release the space bar or switch away from the experiment browser window, the video will pause. The percent of time when the video has been paused over the course of the experiment will be displayed at the top of the screen.
