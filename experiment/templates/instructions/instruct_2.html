Instructions—decision-making task

In the decision making task, you will move clockwise through six contexts, each with a different color. The context map at the bottom of the screen shows your current position as a stick-figure icon, and a counter at the top of the screen shows the number of context visits remain in the task.

Each time you visit a context, you will make a choice and receive an outcome. This outcome corresponds to the number of seconds that you will get to play the arcade game during the following 36-second outcome phase. The details of the outcome phase will be explained later.

In each context, you will choose between two cards: a "best-yet" card on the left, and a "mystery" card on the right. Click a card to select it, and then click the button marked "click for outcome".

The best-yet card always produces the outcome printed on it, and the context map shows the current best-yet card value in each context. The best-yet card starts at a random value between 12 and 18, in multiples of 3. In other words, it starts at 12, 15, or 18.

The mystery card produces a randomly-generated outcome each time it is chosen. One third of the time (33%), it will produce an outcome of 0. Two thirds of the time (67%), it will produce a random outcome between 12 and 36, again in multiples of 3. If the mystery card produces an outcome higher than the current value of the best-yet card, this value will replace the value of best-yet card. This means that while choosing the mystery card can sometimes produce poor outcomes, it can also improve your outcomes in the long run by improving the best-yet card.
