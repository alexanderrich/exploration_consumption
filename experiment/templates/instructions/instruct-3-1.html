Instructions

In the final part of the experiment, you will continue to play the same card game with 6 contexts. But now, instead of simply receiving between 0 and 12 points after each outcome, the points you receive will determine the content of a short outcome phase after each outcome.

Each outcome phase lasts 36 seconds total. For each point you receive, you will get 3 seconds to play a version of the game "breakout". For example, if you receive 4 points, you will get to play breakout for 12 of the 36 seconds.

In breakout, you use your mouse to control the position of a paddle near the bottom of the screen. Your goal is to bounce a ball into rows of bricks to destroy them, without letting the ball fall out of the bottom of the screen. You will gain a point for each brick you destroy, and new bricks will appear when you clear the entire screen.
