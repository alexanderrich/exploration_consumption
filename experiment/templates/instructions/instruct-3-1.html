Instructions

In the final phase of the experiment, you will continue to play the same card game with 6 contexts. But now, instead of the outcome on the chosen card producing points that directly convert to a monetary bonus, the outcome of the card will determine the content of a short outcome phase after each outcome.

Each outcome phase lasts 36 seconds in total, and the time left in the phase will be displayed at the top of the screen. You will get 3 seconds times your chosen card's outcome to play a version of the game "breakout". For example, if you receive an outcome of 4, you will get to play breakout for 12 of the 36 seconds.



In breakout, you use your mouse to control the position of a paddle near the bottom of the screen. Your goal is to bounce a ball into rows of bricks to destroy them, without letting the ball fall out of the bottom of the screen. You will gain a point for each brick you destroy, and new bricks will appear when you clear the entire screen. In addition, when a brick is destroyed it will occasionally produce a "+3" bonus brick that will fall down the screen. Catch this brick with your paddle for an extra 3 points!

At the end of the experiment, points scored in breakout will be converted into a cash bonus using the same rate of 1 cent per 10 points introduced in the previous phase. This bonus will be added to your bonus from the previous phase.
