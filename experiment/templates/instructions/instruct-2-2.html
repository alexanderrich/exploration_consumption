Instructions

On some trials, a set of smaller colored squares will appear on the map between the contexts. When your icon passes one of these squares, you will be asked to make an advanced choice for the context matching that color. This means you will be shown the context matching that color, and be asked to commit to either choosing the best-yet card or the mystery card on your next trial in that context.

After making an advanced choice, a small "a" will appear under that context on the map to indicate that a choice has already been made. When you reach that context, rather than making a choice, you will simply complete the choice you made earlier and receive its outcome. Advanced choices always occur 4 trials ahead of when the context will next be reached.

In this part of the experiment, points you receive from your choices will be converted into a cash bonus received at the end of the experiment. Points will be converted into money at a rate of 1 cent for each 10 points.
