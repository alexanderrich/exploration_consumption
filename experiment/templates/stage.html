  • When you choose a new setting, there is a 1/3 chance that the new setting will fail to be created and the machine will definitely not work. There is a 2/3 chance that a new setting will be created, with a gold wedge that takes up between 1/3 of the spinner and the entire spinner.
  • If you choose a new setting and it is better than the current setting, it will be saved as the current setting for that machine.
  • After using a machine, there is a 1 in 6 chance that the machine's current setting will be reset to a gold wedge between 1/3 and 2/3 of the spinner.
